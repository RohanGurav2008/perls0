About Us

fashion & Designing

Company Profile: pearls Company Name: pearls Established: 2024 Location: PUNE, INDIA, Industry: Ready-Made Garments Type: Private Startup Mission Statement: At pearls , we are dedicated to crafting high-quality ready-made clothing while making a positive social impact. Our garments are designed to reflect the latest fashion trends, and we commit a portion of our profits to charitable initiatives that support underprivileged communities. We believe in fashion with a cause, aiming to merge quality, style, and corporate social responsibility.

Established in 2024, Pearls Ready Mead Garments Company is a forward-thinking brand dedicated to offering high-quality, stylish, and affordable ready-made garments. We specialize in crafting versatile clothing for men, women, and children, catering to a wide range of fashion needs, from everyday wear to special occasions. With our headquarters located in city- pune in INDIA we pride ourselves on combining modern design with timeless craftsmanship. Our skilled team of designers, fabric experts, and craftsmen work together to ensure that every garment reflects the latest trends while maintaining a focus on comfort and durability. Our Vision: To be a leading global brand, offering accessible fashion while setting benchmarks for quality and sustainability in the garment industry. Our Mission: At Pearls Ready Mead Garments, our mission is to provide trendy and durable clothing at competitive prices, ensuring our customers look their best in every season. We aim to deliver superior products while upholding ethical manufacturing practices and minimizing our environmental impact. What We Offer: Diverse Range of Products: From casual everyday wear to formal outfits, our collections are designed to meet the needs of all age groups and occasions. Quality Fabrics: We carefully source premium fabrics to ensure long-lasting, comfortable, and eco-friendly garments. Innovative Designs: Our design team continuously updates our collections, reflecting the latest trends and catering to a variety of styles. Sustainability Commitment: We are dedicated to reducing our carbon footprint by using sustainable materials and adopting eco-friendly production processes.

Core Values Quality: Premium garments designed with attention to detail. Sustainability: Using eco-friendly materials and sustainable production methods. Social Responsibility: Supporting charitable causes and empowering disadvantaged communities. Innovation: Staying ahead of fashion trends through creative designs and production techniques. Customer-Centric Approach: Placing our customers at the heart of everything we do.
